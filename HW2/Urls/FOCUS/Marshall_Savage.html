Marshall Savage

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Marshall Thomas Savage (born 6 August 1955), is an advocate of space travel who wrote The Millennial Project: Colonizing the Galaxy in Eight Easy Steps[1] and founded the First Millennial Foundation,[2] which was designed to make plans for stellar exploration over the next 1,000 years.
