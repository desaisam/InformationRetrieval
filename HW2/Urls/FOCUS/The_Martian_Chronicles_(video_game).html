The Martian Chronicles is a 1996 video game developed by Byron Preiss Multimedia Company, released for Windows and Mac OS.

Gameplay[edit]

The Martian Chronicles is an adventure game based on the works of Ray Bradbury.[1]

Reception[edit]

In 1996, Computer Gaming World declared The Martian Chronicles the 16th-worst computer game ever released.[1]
