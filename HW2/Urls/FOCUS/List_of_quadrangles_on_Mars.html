List of quadrangles on Mars

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The surface of Mars has been divided into 30 quadrangles by the United States Geological Survey, so named because their borders lie along lines of latitude and longitude and so maps appear rectangular. Martian quadrangles are named after local features[1][2] and are numbered with the prefix "MC" for "Mars Chart".[3] West longitude is used.

The thirty cartographic quadrangles of Mars, defined by the United States Geological Survey.[4][5] The quadrangles are numbered with the prefix "MC" for "Mars Chart."[6] Click on a quadrangle name link and you will be taken to the corresponding article. North is at the top; 0°N 180°W﻿ / ﻿0°N 180°W﻿ / 0; -180 is at the far left on the equator. The map images were taken by the Mars Global Surveyor.
