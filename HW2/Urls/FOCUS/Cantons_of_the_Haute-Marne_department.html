Cantons of the Haute-Marne department

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The following is a list of the 17 cantons of the Haute-Marne department, in France, following the French canton reorganisation which came into effect in March 2015:[1]

  • Bologne
  • Bourbonne-les-Bains
  • Chalindrey
  • Châteauvillain
  • Chaumont-1
  • Chaumont-2
  • Chaumont-3
  • Eurville-Bienville
  • Joinville
  • Langres
  • Nogent
  • Poissons
  • Saint-Dizier-1
  • Saint-Dizier-2
  • Saint-Dizier-3
  • Villegusien-le-Lac
  • Wassy
