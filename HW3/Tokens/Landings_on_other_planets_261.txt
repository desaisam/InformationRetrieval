List_of_landings_on_extraterrestrial_bodies
 This is a list of all spacecraft landings on other planets and bodies in the Solar System , including soft landings and both intended and unintended hard impacts . The list includes orbiters that were intentionally crashed, but not orbiters which later crashed in an unplanned manner due to orbital decay . For a list of all planetary missions, including orbiters and flybys, see List of Solar System probes .
