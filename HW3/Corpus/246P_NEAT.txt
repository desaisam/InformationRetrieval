246pneat is a periodic comet discovered on 2004 march 28 by near-earth asteroid tracking neat using the 1.2-metre 47 in reflector at haleakala it was given the permanent number 246p on 2011 january 14it is a quasi-hilda comet due to perturbations by jupiter the 2005 2013 and 2021 perihelion passages will be closer to the sun the comet is observable all through its orbit
