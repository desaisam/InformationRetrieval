178phug–bell is a periodic comet in the solar system it was discovered by northeast kansas amateur astronomers league members gary hug and graham bell and is thought to be the first periodic comet to be discovered by amateurs it was declared a comet less than two days after its initial discovery after having its course confirmed on previous imageshug-bells orbital period is about seven years its orbit is eccentric though less so than many comets hug-bells orbit lies entirely outside the orbit of mars but at its aphelion overlaps in solar distance with the orbit of jupiter because it never comes closer to the sun than about 2 au it is never expected to be a very bright comet with a typical perihelion magnitude of 18-19
