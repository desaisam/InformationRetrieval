rockets from the ariane family have accumulated 246 launches since 1979 234 of which were successful yielding a 95.1% success rate the currently operational version ariane 5 has flown 82 consecutive missions without failure between april 2003 and december 2017 but suffered a malfunction during flight va-241 in january 2018 causing its two satellites to reach an incorrect orbit and reducing their predicted lifetime as they consumed some of their own fuel to raise their orbits
