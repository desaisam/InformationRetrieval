31pschwassmann–wachmann also known as schwassmann–wachmann 2 is a periodic comet in the solar system it was discovered on january 17 1929 at an apparent magnitude of 11 the comet has been seen at every apparition
the comet nucleus is estimated to be 6.2 kilometers in diameter in 1929 the astronomer anne sewell young identified the comet with an object that had been misidentified as the minor planet adelaide a904 eb
