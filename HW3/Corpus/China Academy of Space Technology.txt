the china academy of space technology cast chinese 中国空间技术研究院 pinyin zhōngguó kōngjiān jìshù yánjiū yuàn is a chinese space agency and subordinate of china aerospace science and technology corporation casc the agency was founded on 20 february 1968 and is the main spacecraft development and production facility in china on 24 april 1970 cast successfully launched chinas first artificial satellite dong fang hong i


 space flight programmes 
cast designs and manufactures the dong fang hong satellites
